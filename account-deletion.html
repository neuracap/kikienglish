Account Deletion Guidelines

We believe in giving you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data from Kiki English, please follow these steps:

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Kiki English App.
  2. Go to Settings > Profile.
  3. Tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice. This action is irreversible.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to divyamittal_ias@gmail.com.
  2. Subject: "Request for Account Deletion".
  3. Include your registered email or username in the body.

What Data is Deleted?

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile information.
  • Learning progress and history.
  • Quiz scores and achievements.

Some data may be retained in backup logs for a limited period as required by law or for security purposes.
