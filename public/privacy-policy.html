Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kiki English. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for the app's functionality:

  • Microphone Usage: Used solely for the speech recognition feature to check pronunciation. Audio is processed transiently and not stored permanently.
  • Progress Data: We store local progress (words learned, quiz scores) to track learning.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting (e.g., Firebase) to improve the app stability. These services adhere to strict privacy standards.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information (PII) from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us.
